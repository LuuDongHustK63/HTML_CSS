THE BAND

We love music

Maroon 5 is an American pop rock band from Los Angeles, California. It currently includes lead vocalist Adam Levine, keyboardist and rhythm guitarist Jesse Carmichael, lead guitarist James Valentine, drummer Matt Flynn, keyboardist PJ Morton and multi-instrumentalist Sam Farrar. Although he had to change members (drummer Ryan Dusick left the group in 2006), Maroon 5 continued to succeed thanks to the direction of Jesse Carmicheal, Adam Levine and James Valentine. Maroon 5 has won 3 Grammy Awards along with 5 other nominations. They were one of America's most successful groups in the first decade of the 21st century. Since 2002, the band has sold 10 million albums across the US and 15 million worldwide.
